The Ability Experience

The Ability Camp, the first Philanthropy created by a Fraternity in the United States, is a special initiative focused on supporting individuals with disabilities through inclusive activities. Through this annual event I am able to visit the camp Brenham, Texas with my fraternity brothers and give back. The Ability Camp embodies Pi Kappa Phi's commitment to fostering an inclusive and welcoming community, which aligns with our core values of service and leadership. It is a day I look forward to each year, seeing the smiles on the kids faces as we play games or build bonfires brings warmth to my heart.
